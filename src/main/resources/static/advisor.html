Can you explain how neural networks work?

10:25 AM
AI
ProBot Advisor

Neural networks are computing systems inspired by the human brain. They consist of layers of interconnected nodes (neurons) that process information. Here's a simple breakdown:

  1. Input Layer: Receives the initial data
  2. Hidden Layers: Perform computations and feature extraction
  3. Output Layer: Produces the final result or prediction

Each connection has a weight that adjusts during training to minimize errors. The network learns by adjusting these weights through backpropagation.
